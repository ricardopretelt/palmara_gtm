Privacy & Cookies

Palmara is a fictional brand. This website is a measurement laboratory built to demonstrate GA4 and Google Tag Manager instrumentation. No products are sold, no orders are taken, and nothing you submit here is sent to any business.

What we collect

With your consent, this site uses Google Analytics 4 (via Google Tag Manager) to record anonymous usage events, such as:

  • Pages you view and how you navigate between them.
  • Interactions with buttons, filters, the search bar and forms (event names and context like product line or button location — never the personal details you type).
  • Technical basics such as browser type and screen size.

Consent — how it works

Nothing is tracked until you make a choice in the cookies notice:

  • Accept — analytics storage is granted and usage events are sent to GA4.
  • Decline — analytics remains denied. No events are sent and no analytics cookies are set. Declining means no data, full stop.

Your choice is stored in your browser (local storage key palmara_consent) so we don’t ask again on every page.

Changing your choice

To change or withdraw your consent, clear this site’s data in your browser settings (cookies and site data). The cookies notice will appear again on your next visit.

Forms

The product registration form is a demonstration. Analytics records only the funnel stages (started, progressed, submitted, succeeded) and validation error types — the values you type stay in your browser and are never transmitted or stored.
